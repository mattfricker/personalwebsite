My Work in Security Studies

Summary

Most recently, I co-authored a textbook on drone warfare that is due for publication in September 2015.

I have conducted extensive research on drone strikes and other targeted killings, demonstrated through founding the Center for the Study of Targeted Killing (formerly UMass Drone) in 2009 and co-authoring several journal articles

In 2012-2013, I was a lecturer at UMass Dartmouth, teaching courses in Terrorism Studies and Comparative Politics.
